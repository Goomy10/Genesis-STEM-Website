Who Are We?

Team Genesis is a STEM Racing team based in Western Australia, directed by five Year 10 students who share a passion for the sport. Formerly known as Legacy-Z, Genesis is driven to exceed the success of previous years through every possible means.

Learn More

What is STEM Racing?

  • STEM Racing (formerly F1 in Schools) is the largest international STEM program in the world – involving: 57 countries, 17,000 schools and 1.9 million students.
  • In teams of 3–6, students design, manufacture, and race miniature Formula 1 cars using engineering software and aerodynamic analysis, giving students a hands-on look into the STEM environment
